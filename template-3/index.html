Deploy has evolved.

What began as a private beta to validate demand for clean, market-driven yield revealed something more enduring.

Without marketing, incentives, or favorable market conditions, Deploy's engine demonstrated that stable income from global on-chain perpetual markets can be delivered safely, autonomously, and at scale.

The implication was clear: the engine itself is more valuable than any single consumer interface built on top of it.

Today's rebrand reflects that focus.

Deploy is evolving from a standalone yield product into infrastructure designed to support a new class of digital dollars: assets whose income is driven by real on-chain market activity, not government-compressed rates.

This evolution begins with dUSD, but it does not end there.

As part of this next phase, Deploy is selectively exploring strategic allocations, TVL partnerships, and integration opportunities with aligned counterparties. For inquiries, reach out at hello@deploy.finance.
